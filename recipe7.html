🍩 Medu Vada

Crispy, fluffy lentil donuts - perfect with coconut chutney and sambar

⏱️ Prep: 20 mins + soaking 🔥 Cook: 25 mins 🍽️ Makes: 12 vadas ⭐ Difficulty: Medium

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tbsp rice flour (optional, for crispiness)
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp curry leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander (chopped)
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns (crushed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

👩‍🍳 Procedure

  • Wash urad dal thoroughly and soak in water for 4-5 hours.
  • Drain all water and grind dal with minimal water to a thick, fluffy batter.
  • Batter should be light and airy; when you drop a bit in water, it should float.
  • Add salt, ginger, green chilies, curry leaves, coriander, peppercorns, cumin, and hing. Mix gently.
  • Add rice flour if batter is too runny (should be thick enough to hold shape).
  • Heat oil in a deep pan on medium flame.
  • Wet your palms with water, take a lemon-sized portion of batter and shape into a ball.
  • Make a hole in the center with your thumb to form a donut shape.
  • Gently slide the vada into hot oil.
  • Fry 3-4 vadas at a time, flipping until golden brown and crisp on both sides (4-5 minutes per batch).
  • Drain on paper towels. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Use cold water while grinding for fluffy texture
  • Don't add too much water while grinding; batter should be thick
  • Whisk the batter in one direction for 2-3 minutes to incorporate air
  • Test oil temperature by dropping a small piece of batter; it should rise immediately
  • Add finely chopped onions for extra flavor (optional)
  • Keep your palms wet to prevent batter from sticking

⚠️ Precautions

  • Don't over-grind the dal; it can become sticky and absorb more oil
  • Maintain medium flame; high flame will brown vadas without cooking inside
  • Don't overcrowd the pan while frying
  • Ensure batter is not too runny, or vadas will absorb excess oil
