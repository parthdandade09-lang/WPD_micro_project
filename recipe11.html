🥣 Creamy Pasta

Delicious creamy pasta with your choice of sauce and vegetables

⏱️ Prep: 10 mins 🔥 Cook: 20 mins 🍽️ Serves: 4 ⭐ Difficulty: Easy

🛒 Ingredients

  • 400g pasta (penne, fusilli, or any shape)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (chopped)
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Optional: Cooked chicken or bacon

👩‍🍳 Procedure

  • Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat butter and olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • Add garlic and onions, sauté until soft (2-3 minutes).
  • Add mushrooms and bell peppers, cook for 3-4 minutes until slightly softened.
  • Add cream and milk, stir well. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  • Add Parmesan cheese, oregano, chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Stir until cheese melts.
  • Add cooked pasta and toss to coat. Add reserved pasta water if sauce is too thick.
  • If adding chicken/bacon, add now and mix well.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan. Serve hot.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Save pasta water - it helps create silky smooth sauce
  • Use fresh Parmesan for best flavor (avoid pre-shredded)
  • Add vegetables of your choice - broccoli, spinach, peas work great
  • For lighter version, use half-and-half instead of cream
  • Don't overcook pasta; it continues cooking in sauce
  • Add a pinch of nutmeg for extra depth of flavor

⚠️ Precautions

  • Keep flame low after adding cream to prevent curdling
  • Don't boil sauce vigorously once cream is added
  • Don't rinse pasta after draining; starch helps sauce adhere
  • Add salt gradually; cheese already contains salt
