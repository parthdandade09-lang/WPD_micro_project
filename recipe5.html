🌶️ Schezwan Rice

Spicy, flavorful fried rice with Schezwan sauce and vegetables

⏱️ Prep: 15 mins 🔥 Cook: 20 mins 🍽️ Serves: 3 ⭐ Difficulty: Easy

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old preferred)
  • 2 tbsp Schezwan sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/2 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup spring onions (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • For Schezwan Sauce: 10 dried red chilies (soaked)
  • For Schezwan Sauce: 1 tbsp garlic (chopped)
  • For Schezwan Sauce: 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • For Schezwan Sauce: 1 tsp vinegar
  • For Schezwan Sauce: 1/2 tsp sugar

👩‍🍳 Procedure

  • To make Schezwan sauce, blend soaked red chilies with garlic into a paste.
  • Heat 1 tbsp oil, add chili-garlic paste and sauté for 2 minutes.
  • Add soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and 2 tbsp water. Simmer for 3-4 minutes. Set aside.
  • Heat oil in a wok on high flame. Add ginger-garlic paste, sauté for 30 seconds.
  • Add all vegetables (except spring onions) and stir-fry on high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add Schezwan sauce, soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar. Mix well.
  • Add cooked rice, black pepper, and salt. Toss well to combine.
  • Stir-fry on high heat for 3-4 minutes, tossing continuously.
  • Add spring onions and mix well.
  • Serve hot with Manchurian or chili chicken.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Use day-old refrigerated rice for best texture (prevents mushiness)
  • Keep all ingredients ready before starting as stir-frying is quick
  • Use high heat for authentic Chinese-style fried rice
  • Adjust Schezwan sauce quantity according to spice preference
  • Add paneer or tofu for protein variation
  • Don't overcook vegetables; they should remain crunchy

⚠️ Precautions

  • Use high flame throughout for best results
  • Don't overcrowd the wok; fry in batches if needed
  • Be careful with salt as soy sauce already contains salt
  • Wash and dry vegetables thoroughly before chopping
