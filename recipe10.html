🍕 Pizza

Homemade pizza with gooey cheese, fresh toppings, and crispy crust

⏱️ Prep: 20 mins + rising 🔥 Cook: 15 mins 🍽️ Makes: 2 medium pizzas ⭐ Difficulty: Medium

🛒 Ingredients

  • For Dough: 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • For Dough: 1 tsp instant yeast
  • For Dough: 1 tsp sugar
  • For Dough: 1/2 tsp salt
  • For Dough: 2 tbsp olive oil
  • For Dough: 3/4 cup warm water
  • For Pizza Sauce: 4 tomatoes (pureed)
  • For Pizza Sauce: 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • For Pizza Sauce: 1 tsp oregano
  • For Pizza Sauce: 1 tsp basil
  • For Toppings: 2 cups mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • For Toppings: 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • For Toppings: 1/2 cup onions (sliced)
  • For Toppings: 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • For Toppings: 1/2 cup olives (sliced)
  • For Toppings: 1/2 cup pepperoni or paneer (optional)
  • For Toppings: 2 tbsp olive oil for brushing

👩‍🍳 Procedure

  • Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  • In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead into smooth dough.
  • Cover and let rise in warm place for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  • For sauce: Heat olive oil, sauté garlic until fragrant. Add tomato puree, oregano, basil, salt. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F) with pizza stone or baking tray inside.
  • Punch down dough and divide into 2 portions. Roll into circles on floured surface.
  • Transfer dough to parchment paper. Spread pizza sauce evenly.
  • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously. Add desired toppings.
  • Brush crust edges with olive oil. Slide pizza onto preheated stone/tray.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  • Remove, garnish with fresh basil, and serve hot.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Use bread flour for crispier, chewier crust
  • Preheat oven with pizza stone for at least 30 minutes
  • Don't overload toppings; it makes pizza soggy
  • Use low-moisture mozzarella for better melt
  • Let dough rise in warm place for best texture
  • Brush crust with garlic butter for extra flavor

⚠️ Precautions

  • Don't add too much flour while kneading; dough should be soft
  • Ensure oven is fully preheated before baking
  • Don't open oven door frequently while baking
  • Let pizza cool for 2 minutes before slicing to prevent cheese from sliding
