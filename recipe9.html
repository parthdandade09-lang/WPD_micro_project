🍝 Spaghetti

Classic Italian pasta with rich tomato sauce and fresh basil

⏱️ Prep: 10 mins 🔥 Cook: 25 mins 🍽️ Serves: 4 ⭐ Difficulty: Easy

🛒 Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti pasta
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes (pureed)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Optional: 1 tbsp tomato paste

👩‍🍳 Procedure

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  • Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  • Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large pan. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (30 seconds).
  • Add chopped onions and cook until soft and translucent (3-4 minutes).
  • Add tomato puree, oregano, basil, chili flakes, and salt. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until sauce thickens.
  • If using tomato paste, add now and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Add cooked spaghetti to the sauce. Toss well to coat.
  • Add reserved pasta water gradually to achieve desired consistency.
  • Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Serve immediately while hot.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Use fresh, ripe tomatoes for the best flavor
  • Don't rinse pasta after draining; starch helps sauce stick
  • Add pasta water to sauce - it contains starch that binds everything together
  • Cook pasta al dente (firm to bite) for authentic texture
  • Add a pinch of sugar if tomatoes are too acidic
  • Use good quality olive oil for better taste

⚠️ Precautions

  • Don't overcook pasta; it becomes mushy
  • Salt pasta water generously - it's the only chance to season the pasta itself
  • Don't burn garlic; it becomes bitter
  • Simmer sauce on low flame for best flavor development
